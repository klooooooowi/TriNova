About TriNova

Trinova represents the three core pillars influencing student learning: Home, School, and Students’ Well-being. “Tri” symbolizes these three factors, while “Nova” stands for new insights, strategies, and support for learners.

Our Goal

Trinova aims to bridge research and real-life application. By understanding how parenting styles, household responsibilities, emotional support, and study environments affect Grade 7 and Grade 12 learners at APEC Schools Marikina Heights, Trinova provides practical guidance for students, parents, and educators to help improve academic outcomes.
